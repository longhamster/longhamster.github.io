Variables

  • Business Name
  • Owner Name
  • Business Location
  • Type of License
  • Inspection Date and Results
  • Violation Level and Description

Project Ideas

  • A heat map of Northeastern campus showing colored areas according to intensity of violation level, with ability to hover over specific restaurants to see the violation description
  • A first-person view of a kitchen where user can see "problem" areas and click on them to see what common violations are associated with them and what Boston restaurants have been found with the violation
  • A visual timeline of a specific restaurant showing spikes in violation types and intensity over the course of 5-8 years
